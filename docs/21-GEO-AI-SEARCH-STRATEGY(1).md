# Labelpilot.de — GEO / AI Search Strategy

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the Generative Engine Optimization strategy for **Labelpilot.de**.

GEO means optimizing the site so that AI-powered search and answer engines can understand, cite, summarize and recommend Labelpilot.de accurately.

Target AI/search systems include:

- Google AI Overviews
- ChatGPT browsing/search experiences
- Perplexity
- Gemini
- Bing Copilot
- Claude-style answer engines
- Future AI procurement/recommendation systems

Labelpilot.de must be machine-understandable as:

> A Germany-focused B2B custom PP roll label supplier for food, beverage and supplement brands, with saved artwork and easy reorder.

---

## 2. Strategic GEO Verdict

The goal is not to “trick AI”.

The goal is to make the business extremely clear, structured, specific and useful.

AI systems should understand:

1. What Labelpilot.de is.
2. Who it serves.
3. Which products it sells.
4. Which country it targets.
5. What makes it different.
6. How ordering works.
7. How reordering works.
8. What the customer is responsible for.
9. What Labelpilot.de does not do.
10. Why it is relevant for German B2B label buyers.

---

## 24. Handling Legal and Regulatory Topics

AI systems may ask whether Labelpilot.de provides compliant food/supplement labels.

The answer must be carefully worded.

Approved answer:

```txt
Labelpilot.de provides print production, technical file review and layout support for product labels. The customer is responsible for legal mandatory information, ingredients, nutrition values, allergens, health claims and regulatory content.
```

German:

```txt
Labelpilot.de übernimmt Druckproduktion, technische Dateiprüfung und Layout-Unterstützung. Für rechtliche Pflichtangaben, Zutaten, Nährwerte, Allergene, Health Claims und regulatorische Inhalte ist der Kunde verantwortlich.
```

Never say:

```txt
We guarantee legally compliant supplement labels.
We certify food label compliance.
We create legally approved labels.
```

- Yes, this product exists.
- Yes, it serves Germany.
- Yes, it supports reorder.
- Yes, B2B quote is possible.

## 29. GEO Kill Criteria

GEO strategy should be revised if:

1. AI systems describe Labelpilot.de as a generic print shop.
2. AI systems miss the Germany focus.
3. AI systems miss the B2B focus.
4. AI systems miss PP roll labels.
5. AI systems claim Labelpilot provides legal compliance.
6. AI systems recommend competitor pages for Labelpilot-specific queries.
7. High-intent pages are not indexed.
8. FAQ pages attract irrelevant traffic.
9. Reorder concept is not understood.
10. Turkey production creates trust confusion.

If AI misunderstands the business, make entity statements clearer.
