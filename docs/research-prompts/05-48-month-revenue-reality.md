## Background: What Labelpilot.de Is

Labelpilot.de is a German B2B e-commerce site that launched on 02.06.2026. It sells individually printed PP roll labels (PP-Rollenetiketten), with its main product being 100 x 200 mm PP roll labels. The business is German-only (site language, copy, and targeting). It does NOT sell blank labels.

**Business model specifics:**
- Fixed-price packages: 1,000 / 2,000 / 5,000 / 10,000 labels per order
- Self-serve add-ons per order: design service EUR 40 (waived free if order value >= EUR 2,000 net OR if the customer uploads print-ready artwork); printed proof EUR 10 (optional); express delivery (price TBD); extra design variants (price TBD)
- Repeat orders: saved artwork system — returning customers can reorder with one click
- No Shopify — custom Next.js site with Stripe payment processing
- Seller of record: Zhenkai Global Trading Limited (Hong Kong); DDP fulfillment from Turkey via DHL/UPS to German business addresses
- Target customer segments: food producers, beverage brands, supplement brands, private-label brands, small/medium packaged-product companies — all in Germany (DACH secondary)
- 48-month business target: 1,000 orders/month OR EUR 100,000/month revenue (whichever comes first)
