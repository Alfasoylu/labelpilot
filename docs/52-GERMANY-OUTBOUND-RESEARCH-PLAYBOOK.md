# Labelpilot.de — Germany Outbound Research Playbook

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the Germany outbound research playbook for **Labelpilot.de**.

The goal is to identify German B2B prospects likely to buy recurring PP roll labels.

Target buyers:

```txt
food micro brands
beverage brands
supplement brands
coffee roasters
tea brands
spice brands
honey/jam producers
Shopify D2C product brands
Amazon/Etsy packaged product sellers
```

This is a research playbook, not a spam automation plan.

---

## 2. Strategic Verdict

The correct outbound strategy is:

> Find real German packaged product brands with repeat label need, qualify them, then send relevant low-pressure German outreach.

The wrong strategy is:

> Scrape random emails and spam everyone.

Outbound must be targeted and controlled.

---

## 3. Target Customer Criteria

Good fit:

```txt
Germany-based
physical packaged product
food/beverage/supplement
visible product labels
small or micro brand
likely repeat production
has website/shop
has contact email or form
```

Bad fit:

```txt
service business
no packaging
large enterprise procurement complexity
private hobby project
one-time event product
non-target print need
```

## 9. Outreach Angle

Good German angle (concrete, product-led, no buzzwords):

```txt
Wir produzieren PP-Rollenetiketten (opak/transparent) für Lebensmittel-, Getränke- und Supplement-Marken. Druckdaten werden gespeichert, Nachbestellungen gehen schneller.
```

Do not lead with:

```txt
We are cheapest.
günstigste Lösung / Komplettlösung für Ihre Marke
```

Avoid buzzwords ("innovative Lösung", "Komplettlösung", "Partner für Ihre Marke", "premium Qualität").

Lead with concrete product + repeat order: PP-Rollenetiketten, opak/transparent, gespeicherte Druckdaten, schneller nachbestellen.

Never claim German/local production (production is in Turkey); no unsupported Germany trust claims.

---

## 10. First Outreach Message Principles

Message should be:

```txt
German
short
specific
low pressure
relevant to their product
clear CTA
no fake personalization
no exaggerated claims
```

CTA options (German only, canonical):

```txt
Musterbox anfordern
Angebot anfordern
PP-Etiketten konfigurieren
kurz Etikettenbedarf prüfen
```

Never use English CTAs.

---

## 11. Compliance Warning

Do not automate mass cold email without legal review.

Before outbound email:

```txt
review GDPR/ePrivacy rules
use legitimate interest carefully
avoid scraping abuse
provide opt-out
do not add to newsletter
do not mislead
```

Manual research and targeted outreach first.

---

## 12. Outbound Research Workflow

```txt
identify niche
search prospects
record company data
score prospect
qualify manually
prepare outreach note
contact manually or through compliant tool
record status
follow up only if appropriate
convert to quote/sample/order
```

---

## 13. Weekly Research Target

Initial manual target:

```txt
100 researched prospects/week
30 qualified prospects/week
10 contacted prospects/week
```

Quality beats volume.

If response rate is weak, improve targeting before increasing volume.

---
