## 1. Executive Summary — Top 5 Strategic Findings

**Finding 1: The roll label niche is genuinely uncontested by specialists.**
Every competitor is either a broad general print shop (wir-machen-druck.de, saxoprint.de, vistaprint.de, druck.de) or a sticker/decal shop (typographus.de, aufkleberdrucker24.de, aufkleber-drucken-lassen.de). Not one domain is positioned as a B2B specialist in PP roll labels for food, beverage, or supplement brands. "Etiketten auf rolle" has only wir-machen-druck.de at #1 with 322 traffic from 1.3K monthly searches — a thin, undefended position.

**Finding 2: The five highest-value Labelpilot keywords have no direct specialist competition.**
Rollenetiketten (1K/mo, KD 21), rollenetiketten drucken (1K/mo, KD 25), lebensmitteletiketten (390/mo, KD 10), flaschenetiketten drucken (390–1K/mo, KD 21–22), and etiketten auf rolle (1.3K/mo, KD 20) are all ranked by generalist or sticker sites with thin content. A dedicated label specialist page for each would outrank existing content within a normal SEO build-up timeline.

**Finding 3: Domain authority required to compete is lower than expected.**
The most direct competitors for label-specific keywords have Authority Scores between 13 and 48. aufkleberdrucker24.de (AS 33, 224 referring domains) ranks #2 for lebensmitteletiketten. typographus.de (AS 27, 686 referring domains) holds #1 for rollenetiketten. Labelpilot does not need to build a 600-domain backlink profile to compete — targeted quality links in the printing/food packaging niche will suffice.

**Finding 4: Paid search for label terms is nearly uncontested.**
Of all competitors analyzed, only HERMA has active paid campaigns touching "etiketten bedruckt auf rolle" and "bedruckte etiketten auf rolle" (70 vol each, competition score 1.0, CPC ~€0.79). No competitor is running a focused PPC campaign for rollenetiketten drucken, PP etiketten, lebensmitteletiketten bestellen, or supplement etiketten. The paid search field for Labelpilot's core terms is wide open.

**Finding 5: AI visibility for label printing is a first-mover opportunity.**
The highest AI Visibility score in this dataset belongs to wir-machen-druck.de (55/100 DE) — but their AI mentions are dominated by coupon/discount queries, not label expertise. No competitor has established authority in AI systems (ChatGPT, Gemini, AI Overview) for roll label printing, food label requirements, or supplement label compliance. Labelpilot can become the cited source for these topics before any competitor invests in this direction.
