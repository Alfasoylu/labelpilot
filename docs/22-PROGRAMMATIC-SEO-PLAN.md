# Labelpilot.de — Programmatic SEO Plan

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the programmatic SEO plan for **Labelpilot.de**.

Labelpilot.de is a German-language, Germany-focused, B2B-first custom PP roll label ordering and reorder platform.

Programmatic SEO must help Labelpilot.de scale German commercial landing pages without creating thin, duplicate or low-quality content.

The goal is to capture qualified German B2B demand for:

- PP-Rollenetiketten
- Lebensmitteletiketten
- Supplement-Etiketten
- Getränkeetiketten
- transparente PP-Etiketten
- opake PP-Etiketten
- Etiketten 100×200 mm
- Branchenetiketten
- Material/Größe/Branche combinations
- Nachbestellung use cases

Programmatic SEO is allowed only if each page is useful, unique and conversion-focused.

---

## 2. Strategic Verdict

The correct programmatic SEO strategy is:

> Controlled German B2B page matrix around industry × material × size × use case, with unique content, product specs, FAQs, internal links and quote/sample CTAs.

The wrong strategy is:

> Mass-generate thousands of near-duplicate pages and hope Google indexes them.

Thin pages will damage the domain.

Controlled, useful pages can build category authority.


## 7. Page Quality Gate

A programmatic page may be published only if it has:

1. Unique H1.
2. Unique title.
3. Unique meta description.
4. Unique direct answer block.
5. Unique use-case explanation.
6. Product specification table.
7. Material/size relevance.
8. At least 4 useful FAQs.
9. Internal links.
10. Quote CTA.
11. Sample box CTA if relevant.
12. Reorder explanation where relevant.
13. Compliance disclaimer if food/beverage/supplement.
14. Schema matching visible content.
15. No duplicate body content.

If a page cannot meet these rules, do not create it.

## 13. FAQ Template

Each programmatic page must include 4–6 specific FAQs.

Example for supplement + transparent PP:

```txt
Sind transparente PP-Etiketten für Supplement-Dosen geeignet?
Wann ist transparentes PP besser als opakes PP?
Kann ich dieselben Supplement-Etiketten später nachbestellen?
Welche Druckdateien werden akzeptiert?

```

Answers must be specific to the page.

Do not copy-paste the same FAQ across every page without adaptation.

## 14. Compliance Disclaimer

For food, beverage and supplement-related pages, include:

```txt
Hinweis: Für rechtliche Pflichtangaben, Zutaten, Nährwerte, Allergene, Health Claims und regulatorische Inhalte ist der Kunde verantwortlich. Labelpilot.de übernimmt Druckproduktion, technische Dateiprüfung und Layout-Unterstützung, jedoch keine rechtliche Prüfung.
```

This is mandatory.

Never write:

```txt
rechtssichere Etiketten garantiert
EU-konforme Etiketten garantiert
rechtlich geprüfte Supplement-Etiketten
```

Example:


Do not canonical programmatic pages to parent unless the page is intentionally not indexable.

## 18. Indexing Rules

Programmatic page should be indexable only if it passes quality gate.

Indexable:

```txt
unique useful content
clear search intent
commercial relevance
internal links
metadata
schema
```

Noindex:

```txt
draft page
thin page
duplicate page
experimental page
filter/sort page
```

Do not let draft programmatic pages enter sitemap.

---

## 26. Programmatic Expansion Criteria

Only expand beyond first 15 pages if:

1. Pages are indexed.
2. Pages get impressions.
3. Content quality is strong.
4. Internal links work.
5. No duplicate/canonical issues.
6. At least some commercial engagement exists.
7. Search Console shows relevant queries.
8. No thin content warnings.
9. Page template produces useful pages.

If not, improve existing pages before adding more.

---

## 27. Kill Criteria

Stop or pause programmatic SEO if:

1. Pages are not indexed.
2. Google chooses wrong canonicals.
3. Pages get irrelevant impressions.
4. No commercial clicks.
5. Duplicate content issues appear.
6. Pages sound auto-generated.
7. Content drifts into generic printing.
8. Legal overclaims appear.
9. Internal links become messy.
10. Programmatic pages dilute product focus.

---

## 28. Common Mistakes to Avoid

Do not:

1. Generate hundreds of pages at once.
2. Create city pages without local operation.
3. Publish pages without unique content.
4. Duplicate FAQ across every page.
5. Use English URLs.
6. Add non-core print products.
7. Forget compliance disclaimers.
8. Forget CTAs.
9. Forget internal links.
10. Add pages to sitemap before QA.
11. Use keyword stuffing.
12. Overpromise legal compliance.
