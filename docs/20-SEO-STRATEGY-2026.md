# Labelpilot.de — SEO Strategy 2026

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the SEO strategy for **Labelpilot.de**.

Labelpilot.de is a Germany-focused B2B-first label ordering and reorder platform.

The SEO goal is not to attract random print buyers.

The SEO goal is to attract German micro and small brands that need repeat product labels, especially in:

- Food
- Beverage
- Supplements
- Coffee
- Tea
- Spices
- Honey
- Jam
- Bottled products
- Jarred products
- Pouch-packaged products

This document is the source of truth for Codex when implementing SEO-related routes, metadata, structured data, content architecture and internal linking.

---

## 2. Strategic SEO Verdict

Labelpilot.de must not compete as a generic online print shop.

The correct SEO position is:

> B2B custom PP roll labels for German food, beverage and supplement brands.

The wrong SEO position is:

> Cheap stickers, business cards, flyers and generic printing.

Generic print SEO is too competitive, too broad, and too low-margin.

The SEO strategy must be narrow, commercial, German-first, and reorder-focused.

## 3. SEO Business Objective

SEO must support these business outcomes:

| Business Goal | SEO Function |
|---|---|
| Sell 5,000+ PP label packages | Rank commercial landing pages |
| Generate B2B quote requests | Build high-intent service pages |
| Increase trust in Turkey production | Publish transparent logistics and quality content |
| Build repeat customers | Rank reorder and use-case pages |
| Reduce CAC | Capture organic commercial intent |
| Become category authority | Build glossary, comparison and FAQ content |
| Support GEO / AI search | Create structured, direct-answer content |

SEO is not a blog project.

SEO is a sales acquisition system.

## 4. Primary SEO Language

Primary customer-facing SEO language:

```txt
German
```

Secondary future language:

```txt
English
```

Turkish is internal only.

MVP should be German-first.

Preferred URL prefix:

```txt
/de/
```

Example:

```txt
/de/supplement-etiketten
/de/lebensmittel-etiketten
/de/transparente-pp-etiketten
```

Campaign / Ads landing pages use a separate path family:

```txt
/lp/[slug]
```

Rules for `/lp/[slug]` pages:

1. Content must still be German.
2. They are campaign landing pages, not organic SEO winners.
3. They must remain `noindex`.
4. They must not be added to sitemap.
5. They must not become canonical winners over `/de/*` SEO pages.

## 18. Compliance Disclaimer SEO Rule

Pages targeting food, beverage or supplement labels must include a compliance disclaimer.

German copy:

```txt
Hinweis: Für rechtliche Pflichtangaben, Zutaten, Nährwerte, Allergene, Health Claims und regulatorische Inhalte ist der Kunde verantwortlich. Labelpilot.de übernimmt Druckproduktion, technische Dateiprüfung und Layout-Unterstützung, jedoch keine rechtliche Prüfung.
```

This protects the business and avoids misleading SEO claims.

Do not claim:

- legally approved labels
- compliant supplement labels
- certified food labels
- EU legal review included

Unless verified legal service is added later.

Every public page must have a canonical URL.

Product configuration variants should not create duplicate indexable pages unless intentionally designed.

## 26. Core Web Vitals Rules

The site must be fast.

Rules:

1. Optimize images.
2. Avoid large client-side bundles.
3. Use server components where possible.
4. Lazy-load non-critical sections.
5. Avoid unnecessary animations.
6. Keep product pages lightweight.
7. Do not load heavy design editor in MVP.
8. Use font optimization.
9. Avoid blocking third-party scripts.
10. Track performance on mobile.

SEO pages must not depend on heavy client-side rendering.

## 30. SEO Kill Criteria

SEO strategy should be reviewed if:

1. P0 pages are not indexed after technical fixes.
2. Pages get impressions but no clicks.
3. Pages get clicks but no quote/sample conversions.
4. Organic traffic is mostly irrelevant.
5. Blog pages attract consumers but not B2B buyers.
6. Generic sticker keywords dominate traffic.
7. Conversion rate from organic stays below target.
8. Content becomes too broad.
9. Competitor comparison pages do not generate leads.
10. Search Console shows duplicate/canonical problems.

If SEO brings the wrong customers, narrow the page targeting.
